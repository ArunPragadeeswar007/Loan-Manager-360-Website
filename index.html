Features

Everything You Need to
Master Your Finances

Powerful tools designed to give you complete visibility and control over your loan portfolio.

Enterprise-Grade Security

Bank-level encryption powered by Supabase with Row Level Security (RLS), ensuring your financial data remains private and protected at all times.

Loan Lifecycle Management

Track every loan from origination to closure. Monitor active, closed, and upcoming loans with real-time status updates and automated lifecycle tracking.

Informative Dashboards

Beautifully designed analytics dashboards with real-time portfolio insights, visual breakdowns, and comprehensive financial summaries at a glance.

Smart Calculations

Advanced EMI calculations, interest breakdowns, and tenure analysis powered by intelligent algorithms. Get instant insights into your repayment journey.

Multi-Device Experience

Seamlessly access your portfolio from any device. Fully responsive design optimized for desktop, tablet, and mobile with real-time sync across platforms.

Modern Tech Stack

Built with React, TypeScript, Chakra UI, and Supabase — delivering blazing-fast performance, type safety, and a polished premium experience.
